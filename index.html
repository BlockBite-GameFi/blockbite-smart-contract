BlockBiteToken Vesting & Milestone Rewards

Trustless, automated token distributions on Solana. Replace manual transfers with on-chain schedules enforced by smart contracts — no oversight gaps, no exploits.

Quick Start (5 min)
Integration Guide
View on Devnet →
⏱️

Cliff + Linear Vesting

Configure cliff periods and linear unlock schedules enforced entirely on-chain. Four vesting modes from two parameters — no off-chain execution needed.

🎯

Milestone-Gated Streams

Lock tokens until a creator-confirmed milestone is reached. Perfect for team grants tied to product launches, sales targets, or governance votes.

🎮

Campaign & Game Rewards

Game publishers deposit a budget on-chain. Players earn tokens by hitting in-game targets, verified by a trusted game-server oracle.

🔒

Trustless Escrow

All tokens are held in PDA-owned escrow accounts. Neither creator nor recipient can unilaterally move funds — only the program logic can.

⚖️

Prorated Cancellation

Cancel any stream at any time. Vested tokens go to the recipient, unvested tokens return to the creator — calculated and settled atomically on-chain.

🧾

Rent Recovery

After a stream is fully settled, close_stream reclaims ~0.004 SOL rent from both the stream and escrow accounts.
